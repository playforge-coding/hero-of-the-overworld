# Gareth

A **mountain hermit** of [Traveller's End](../world.md#the-world-map) — and, before
that, the **king's own scout**. Gareth saw the advisor treating with demons in the
dark and warned the authorities; for his trouble he was **branded the traitor** and
hunted, so he fled to the high passes and lived alone. He isn't in the starting
party — he steps out of hiding in the level-clear
[cutscene](../battles.md#story-and-cutscenes) once you've fought your way over
Traveller's End, and, learning your party marches on the advisor at last, joins for
the rest of the run.

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| **HP** | 94 |
| **MP** | 30 |
| **Attack** | 20 |
| **Defense** | 11 |
| **Magic** | 8 |
| **Speed** | 21 |
| **Role** | Fast physical striker |
| **Gear** | [Scout's Edge](../equipment.md) + [Scout's Cloak](../equipment.md) |
| **Joins** | After clearing Traveller's End (via the `gareth_joins` cutscene) |

Gareth is the **fastest, nimblest hero in the party** — his speed of 21 towers over
Roland's 12 and Elara's 13, so he almost always acts first, and his gear stacks even
more speed, crit, and evasion on top. He hits reliably rather than heavily: a strong
attack, cheap darting skills, and enough HP to hold the line, but low magic and only
middling defense. Open a round with him to soften or pick off a foe before it can
move, then let Roland tank and Elara nuke.

His scout's reflexes also give him a **far more forgiving
[timing window](../battles.md#action-timing-strikes-and-blocks)** than the rest of
the party — landing **GOOD** and **PERFECT** taps is much easier with Gareth, on
**both his strikes** (for +50% / +100% damage) **and his blocks** (to shrug off
incoming hits). His already-quick blade hits even harder, and he braces more
reliably, in practised hands.

Because a mid-game recruit [joins at the party's current level](../gameplay.md#your-party),
Gareth arrives already levelled up to match Roland — he's a ready front-liner, not a
level-1 straggler you have to grind back up.

## Skills

| Skill | Kind | MP | Target | Learned |
| ----- | ---- | -- | ------ | ------- |
| QUICK SLASH | Physical | 3 | One enemy | Start |
| SWALLOW CUT | Physical | 7 | All enemies | **Level 2** |
| FINAL CUT | Physical | 10 | One enemy | **Level 7** |

**QUICK SLASH** is a cheap, spammable single strike — fitting the quickest blade in
the party; **SWALLOW CUT**, a flowing sweep that flits between every foe (great
against a pack), is learned at **level 2**; and **FINAL CUT** — a flawless killing
stroke and the single hardest-hitting move in the party — is his **last-unlocked**
ultimate at **level 7**. He has no healing; that's [Elara](elara.md)'s alone. Since
Gareth is recruited late, he joins already knowing everything his level grants. See
[Battles](../battles.md#skills) and [Levelling up](../gameplay.md#levelling-up).

## Gear

Gareth keeps the tools of his old trade as a scout: a **[Scout's Edge](../equipment.md)**
— a slim, back-slung blade that adds a little **speed** on top of its keen **crit**
and **accuracy** — and a **[Scout's Cloak](../equipment.md)** that trades heavy
armor for **speed** and a lot of **evasion**, the gear of a man who lived by not
being caught.

## Appearance

Gareth uses the `hermit` sprite sheet — **Roland's `swordsman` layout with the four
unused bottom rows trimmed off**, a 6×8 grid of 16×16 frames. The walk rows (0–3,
down/up/right/left) carry him around the overworld if he leads the party, and the
attack rows (4–7, same directions) play his fast blade-work in battle. He wears his
sword slung across his back.
